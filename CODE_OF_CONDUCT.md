# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be respectful, be constructive, assume good faith. Harassment,
personal attacks and discriminatory behavior are not tolerated anywhere in
the project's spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions).

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer at
<adrianoamalfi@gmail.com>. All complaints will be reviewed promptly and
fairly, and the privacy of reporters will be respected.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce the Code of Conduct may be
permanently removed from the project team.
