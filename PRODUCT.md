# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Mobile-first editorial readers visiting a Ghost publication. They scan the homepage, open posts, browse tags/authors, and decide whether to subscribe or sign in.

## Product Purpose

Astrix Ghost presents an expressive editorial publication theme. Success means readers can understand the publication, navigate posts, and reach subscription actions quickly on mobile without visual clutter or layout friction.

## Brand Personality

Bold, editorial, direct. The interface should feel image-led and confident while staying fast and legible.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page polish, decorative card grids, oversized rounded panels, repeated tiny eyebrow labels, and mobile menus that feel like cramped desktop navigation.

## Design Principles

1. Mobile reading first.
2. Navigation must be obvious and thumb-friendly.
3. Typography can be expressive, but never at the cost of fit or legibility.
4. Imagery and post metadata should support scanning, not compete with titles.
5. Subscription and account actions must remain reachable without dominating the editorial surface.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain readable contrast, 44px touch targets where possible, keyboard-operable navigation, visible focus states, reduced-motion support, and resilient layouts for long labels and author names.
